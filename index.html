LiGHT Sindri was established in May 2021 at Sindri, Dhanbad, and run and organized by students of Birsa Institute of Technology (BIT), Sindri. LiGHT Sindri has been nurtured from seed to a small plant and still has a long way to go to become a fully-fledged tree with multiple purposes.

LiGHT Sindri, an expansion initiative of Gopali Youth Welfare Society with the aim to solve the untouched issues of society is working to its full potential.
